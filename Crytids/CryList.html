GoatMan

A towering humanoid with the muscular build of a man and the twisted horns and shaggy legs of a goat, the Goat Man lurks in the shadows of deep forests and abandoned roads. His glowing eyes pierce the darkness as he mimics human voices to lure unsuspecting travelers into his domain.

Dover Demon

The Dover Demon is a small, humanoid cryptid reportedly sighted in Dover, Massachusetts, in 1977. It is described as having a thin body, long limbs, glowing orange eyes, and a large, smooth head with no visible nose or mouth.
